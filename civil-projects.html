Civil Projects

FLL Airport Gate 100/101 - Broward County Aviation Department

Designed and provided the civil drawings and cost estimates for a project meant to improve the overall security of Gate 100 and 101 with additional closed circuit television cameras and lighting at the Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport. The project consisted of infrastructure site work, lighting and CCTV infrastructure, site restoration and site furnishings. CCTV cameras and associated devices including materials, hardware, software, licensing, warrantees, maintenance, shop drawings and submittals were purchased/provided and installed to support the Closed Circuit Television System at the Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport.

North Perry Airport Taxiway Parking Lot - Broward County Aviation Department

Implemented Broward County Aviation Department comments for the pavement restoration and pavement markings improvements to the Taxiway P runway for the North Perry Airport. The project was part of a program in support of the maintenance and rehabilitation of the airfields in the State of Florida.

State Revolving Fund Report - City of Opa-locka

Designed and provided the preliminary drawings, quantities and cost estimates for the planning of city-wide utility upgrades for the City of Opa-locka. The project included the replacement of extensive storm water, sewer water, and drinking water infrastructure as well as the pavement milling and resurfacing.

Multipurpose Athletic and Wellness Education Center – Florida Memorial University

Designed and provided the civil site development drawings for a complete renovation of a gymnasium and an indoor pool. The proposed building and site work included new sanitary sewer and potable water distribution as well as paving, grading and drainage design.

Shenandoah Neighborhood Design Build Project – Miami Dade County Water and Sewer Department

Created the preliminary civil drawings and compiled all the existing utility information for a design-build neighborhood renovation project. The project replaced the existing undersized and deteriorated water mains and water services with new 8-inch pipes and water services.

FLL Airport East Perimeter Road 16-Inch Water Main Improvements - Broward County Aviation Department

Implemented Broward County Aviation Department comments for a new water main project for the Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport.

FLL Airport East Perimeter Road 16-Inch Water Main Improvements - Broward County Aviation Department

Project Designer responsible for implementation of Broward’s County Aviation Department comments for a new water main project for the Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport.
