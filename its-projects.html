SR 821 Homestead Extension for Florida’s Turnpike (HEFT) Campbell Drive Interchange Improvements Design-Build Project - FPID No. 435462-1-52-01

Project Designer. The project required the design, widening, and reconstruction of the HEFT (SR 821) and Campbell Drive to accommodate the future needs for capacity, operational and safety improvements. In addition the erection of noise walls and signalization improvements of drainage, lighting and ITS were included within the scope. Designed and submitted ITS plans for the ITS infrastructure upgrades and relocation on conflicting devices. Client: Florida’s Turnpike Enterprise, Start/End Dates: 2016-2017

Designed and provided ITS drawings for the installation of fifteen toll related Dynamic Messaging Signs along with their relevant network and power components along the I-95 Express Lanes. Client: FDOT District 6

System wide Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) Cabinet Inspection Services - FDOT District 6
